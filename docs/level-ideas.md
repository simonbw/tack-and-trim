# Level Location Ideas

**British Virgin Islands**
Classic sailing destination, and the core cruising area around Tortola/Virgin Gorda/Jost Van Dyke is surprisingly compact — maybe 15 miles across. Trade winds, sheltered channels between islands, coral reefs to navigate around.

**Bay of Islands (New Zealand)**
About 144 islands in a tight bay, very manageable area. Beautiful and not overdone in games.

**Stockholm Archipelago** (a slice of it)
Thousands of islands, but you could carve out a small section. The narrow channels between rocky granite islands would make for interesting navigation gameplay.

## Mediterranean

**Cyclades (Greece)**
White-washed villages on volcanic islands, the Meltemi wind funneling between islands. Iconic and visually stunning.

**Dalmatian Coast (Croatia)**
Long skinny islands running parallel to the mainland creating sheltered channels. Medieval walled towns like Dubrovnik. Very popular real-world sailing charter area.

**Maddalena Archipelago (Sardinia)**
Tiny cluster of granite islands with turquoise water between Sardinia and Corsica. The Strait of Bonifacio is notoriously windy.

## Northern / Cold Water

**Lofoten Islands (Norway)**
Dramatic fjords, jagged peaks rising straight from the sea, narrow channels. Northern lights potential. Visually jaw-dropping.

**Faroe Islands**
Moody, windswept, vertical sea cliffs. Challenging conditions, very atmospheric.

**Inside Passage (Alaska/BC)**
Glaciers calving into the water, whales, narrow fjord channels with serious tidal currents. Epic scale.

## Tropical

**Whitsunday Islands (Australia)**
Great Barrier Reef sailing, white silica beaches, warm turquoise water. Compact island group.

**Grenadines (Caribbean)**
Tobago Cays, Bequia, Mustique — a chain of small islands with consistent trade winds. Classic bareboat charter territory.

**Raja Ampat (Indonesia)**
Thousands of tiny karst limestone islands covered in jungle, rising out of deep blue water. Otherworldly looking.

**Palau**
Rock islands, jellyfish lakes, WWII wrecks. Very unique visual palette.

## Unique / Unexpected

**Ha Long Bay (Vietnam)**
Thousands of towering limestone karsts jutting from emerald water. Tight maneuvering, dramatic silhouettes.

**Tierra del Fuego (Chile/Argentina)**
End of the world sailing. The Beagle Channel, glaciers, howling winds, Drake Passage nearby. Hardcore.

**Zanzibar Archipelago**
Dhow sailing, spice trade history, coral reefs, monsoon winds that reverse seasonally.

**Thousand Islands (St. Lawrence River, NY/Ontario)**
River sailing through a maze of islands, some barely bigger than a house. Castles on private islands.

**Lake Titicaca (Peru/Bolivia)**
High-altitude lake at 12,500 feet with islands, reed boats, thin air. Would be a wild unique level.

**Svalbard**
Pack ice, polar bears, midnight sun. Extreme expedition-style sailing.
